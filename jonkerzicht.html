The Property

Garden walls.
Your own
world.

Jonkerzicht sits on Merriman Avenue in the quiet residential streets of Simonswyk — one of Stellenbosch's most character-rich neighbourhoods, and well within walking distance of Stellenbosch University's main gate. The property's defining feature is its private walled garden: a contained outdoor world that most student accommodation simply cannot offer.

Five-bedroom apartments with en-suite rooms give each tenant privacy where it counts, while the shared kitchen, living area, and covered braai terrace create the kind of social infrastructure that turns a house into a home. The braai terrace has become something of a landmark for residents — a reliable gathering point across every season of the academic year.

Water, a washing machine, and curtains are included in the rental price. Garden service runs once a fortnight. Jonkerzicht is managed by Gradspace, which means maintenance is handled promptly and the standard of living is maintained consistently throughout your tenancy.
