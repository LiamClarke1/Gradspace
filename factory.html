Situated on Soeteweide Road in Universiteits Oord, The Factory is one of Gradspace's defining properties. High ceilings, generous floor plans, and spaces that carry the character of the building's original form — refined into a living environment that students choose, and return to.

The Factory offers three-, four-, and six-bedroom shared apartments, each with en-suite bedrooms and a fully equipped communal kitchen and living area. The building's industrial bones — exposed finishes, volume, and proportion — give each unit a sense of architectural identity that generic student housing simply cannot offer.

Communal areas are serviced three times a week. Fibre Wi-Fi is included. A dishwasher and laundry machines come standard. The result is a home that asks very little of you beyond being present — which is exactly where your energy belongs.
