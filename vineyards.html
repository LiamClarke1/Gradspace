Space to live.

The Vineyards offers something most student accommodation cannot: genuine breathing room. Situated on the outskirts of Stellenbosch, 2.9km from the Neelsie Student Centre, this property sits in a farm-side setting where open sky, vine rows, and natural quiet replace the noise of town living. It's a different choice — and for the right student, it's the right one.

The property features en-suite bedrooms and well-equipped communal areas. The distance from campus requires a car or bicycle, but it also means that evenings are genuinely peaceful — no town-centre noise, no passing traffic, just the kind of stillness that makes studying and unwinding equally effective.

Fibre Wi-Fi, dishwasher, and laundry facilities are included. Communal cleaning runs three times per week. The Vineyards is managed by Gradspace, with the same maintenance standards applied across every property in the portfolio.
